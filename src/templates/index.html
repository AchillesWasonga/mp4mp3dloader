What To Expect

Success

You will see the saved video path, any metadata path, and the destination folder.

Instagram restrictions

If Instagram refuses public access, choose safari or chrome and make sure that browser is logged into Instagram.

Unavailable source

If the reel or video is gone at the source, the downloader cannot recover it. A new URL is required.
